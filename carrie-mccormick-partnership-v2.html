The Systems — In Your Words

Every module below is something you named. Here's what each one does.

ListingStory · Stage 1 — already live

"A client intake form that auto-generates a polished feature sheet / listing copy."

Your branded seller-intake app. The moment a seller submits, Claude drafts the Property Story — MLS remarks plus an Instagram caption, in your voice and Fair-Housing compliant. Every submission feeds your dashboard and the database the rest of the stack is built on.

Call & Text Concierge · Stage 2

"Need inbound calls and texts automatically captured, transcribed, and recapped into a daily digest instead of living on my phone."

Inbound calls and texts are captured, transcribed, and rolled into one daily digest you actually read — so nothing lives on your phone and nothing slips because you were showing a property when it came in.

Follow-Up Engine · Stage 2

"A system that proactively tells me who to follow up with and when, based on last contact and deal stage."

A proactive engine that surfaces the right name at the right moment — ranked by last contact and deal stage — and drafts the outreach in your voice.

Newsletter Engine · Stage 2

"A recurring newsletter program… market updates, neighborhood spotlights, just-listed/just-sold… drafted in my voice and sent on a reliable cadence."

Market updates, neighborhood spotlights, and just-listed / just-sold features drafted in your voice on a dependable schedule — sent through your existing Mailerlite, so your list and your sending reputation stay yours.

Lead Intelligence Engine · Stage 3

"Connect CRM + social to surface life events… a way to identify divorce or any life-changing moments… match the buyers with properties that come on the market."

Two jobs, one data layer. Life-event monitoring watches connected sources for the moments that move people — births, marriages, graduations, job changes, and other life events — so you can reach out at the right time. Buyer↔listing matching takes each buyer's wish-list and matches it against new and pre-market listings from your MLS / Zenlist data, ranking the fits and drafting the outreach.

Buyer↔listing matching also depends on your MLS / Zenlist data-license terms, which your managing broker must confirm.
