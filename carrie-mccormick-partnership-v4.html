The Systems — In Your Words

Every module below is something you named. Here's what each one does.

ListingStory · already live

"A client intake form that auto-generates a polished feature sheet / listing copy."

Your branded seller-intake app. The moment a seller submits, Claude drafts the Property Story — MLS remarks plus an Instagram caption, in your voice, designed with Fair Housing guardrails. You approve every word before it's used. Every submission feeds your dashboard and the database the rest of the stack is built on.

Follow-Up Engine · ships in your Sprint

"A system that proactively tells me who to follow up with and when, based on last contact and deal stage."

A proactive engine that surfaces the right name at the right moment — ranked by last contact and deal stage — and drafts the outreach in your voice for your approval. Version 1 goes live during your Roadmap Sprint, working off your existing contacts.

Call & Text Concierge

"Need inbound calls and texts automatically captured, transcribed, and recapped into a daily digest instead of living on my phone."

Inbound calls and texts are captured, transcribed, and rolled into one daily digest you actually read — so nothing lives on your phone and nothing slips because you were showing a property when it came in. Built consent-first: Illinois requires all parties to consent to call recording, so the Concierge ships with a caller disclosure notice built in — deployment is conditioned on that consent mechanism being live.

Newsletter Engine

"A recurring newsletter program… market updates, neighborhood spotlights, just-listed/just-sold… drafted in my voice and sent on a reliable cadence."

Market updates, neighborhood spotlights, and just-listed / just-sold features drafted in your voice on a dependable schedule — sent through your existing Mailerlite, so your list and your sending reputation stay yours.

Lead Intelligence Engine

"Connect CRM + social to surface life events… life-changing moments… match the buyers with properties that come on the market."

Two jobs, one data layer. Life-event monitoring watches connected sources for the moments that move people — births, marriages, graduations, job changes, and other life events — so you can reach out at the right time. Buyer↔listing matching takes each buyer's wish-list and matches it against new and pre-market listings from your MLS / Zenlist data, ranking the fits and drafting the outreach for your approval.

Important — please read. Some life-event signals (for example, certain court or probate filings) may be available as public record. Bogen.ai is not a law firm and does not provide legal advice. You and your brokerage are solely responsible for independently confirming the legality and compliance of any public-records-based outreach under Illinois and federal law — including Fair Housing, advertising, and anti-solicitation rules — with your own counsel before any such outreach is used. Bogen.ai does not provide consumer reports, and no system may be used for any FCRA-regulated purpose. Any automated text or call outreach is additionally subject to federal telemarketing rules (TCPA / Do-Not-Call), which require recipient consent that you and your brokerage are responsible for — Bogen.ai builds approval gating into every send, and you approve all outreach in advance. Buyer↔listing matching also depends on your MLS / Zenlist data-license terms, which your managing broker must confirm.

Social Presence Engine · add-on

"Help me post, comment, and stay visible consistently without it feeling generic."

Consistent posting and engagement across YouTube, LinkedIn, Instagram and Facebook, drafted in your written style and brand voice — you approve before anything publishes.
