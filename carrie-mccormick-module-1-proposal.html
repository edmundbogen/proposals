Where this started

What You Told Us

On our July 16 call and your July 17 email, you asked to start with the feature sheets — one focused system, delivered fast, that takes real work off your plate. That is exactly what Module 1 is. Nothing here requires you to learn software. Your sellers fill out one beautiful form; everything below follows automatically.

Scope

What Module 1 Includes

1 · The Seller Intake

  • You've already seen the working system. Production go-live follows a final hardening pass (security, privacy notice, and seller consent flow) in Week 1 — then it's ready for real sellers.
  • Branded in your palette, mobile-ready, elegant enough for your most discerning sellers.
  • Captures the property's facts AND its story: upgrades, features, what the owners love about living there.
  • Each submission is stored in your secure database, emailed to you as a notification, and available in your dashboard.

2 · Monday.com Sync

  • New intakes flow into your Monday.com board automatically — no retyping, no copy-paste.
  • Field mapping is agreed with you in a short setup call so it fits how you already work.
  • Monday.com is a third-party service; we integrate with it but don't control it.

3 · The Property Story — AI Drafting Engine

  • The instant a seller submits, the system drafts listing remarks and a social caption — in your luxury voice, ready for your review.
  • Drafts are screened by an automated Fair-Housing word filter (federal, Illinois, and Chicago protected classes) designed to support your review. You review and approve all copy before use — final compliance always rests with you, as it does with any copy you publish.
  • Your seller sees their home's Property Story appear on screen the moment they hit submit — a moment that makes you look like the most technologically advanced agent they've ever hired.

4 · The Feature Sheet — the deliverable you asked for

  • A designed, branded feature-sheet PDF generated from the same intake — ready for your review, then print and share.

5 · The Listing Page

  • Every property launched through ListingStory gets its own branded landing page: your bio and team information, the property's story, its facts, and its photographs — assembled automatically, polished by us.
  • Photos arrive through a simple upload link you or your photographer opens on any phone.
  • Hosted, secured, and maintained by us. Typically live within one business day of the intake, photos, and your approval being in.

6 · The Scoreboard — page analytics

  • Each listing page reports basic traffic metrics — visits, engagement, and traffic sources where available.
  • You get a clean report you can share with your seller: "here is your home's online activity this week."

Acceptance means the earlier of: (a) your written approval that the system has passed an end-to-end test with one sample property (intake → Property Story → feature sheet → listing page → Monday.com sync); or (b) five business days after we notify you the system is live and available for testing, if no specific, reproducible defect has been identified in writing. Minor cosmetic items don't delay Acceptance — they're handled in ordinary support.

Monthly service — $395/mo begins on the Acceptance date, billed monthly in advance by Stripe invoice (ACH or card, auto-charge authorized).

Invoices unpaid 10 days past due accrue 1.5%/month; service may be suspended on 5 days' written notice until cured (suspension is not termination).
