The Plan

What We'll Build Together

Phase 1 — Foundation

Platform & Positioning

Before building anything new, we need to establish CVL as a standalone, professional platform — separate from your consulting site, with proper security, analytics, and SEO architecture. This is the baseline that earns trust from the senior practitioners you're targeting. Your content is already better than the competition. Your site needs to match.

  • Competitive analysis and positioning strategy — where CVL fits in the FinOps landscape and how to differentiate
